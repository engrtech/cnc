M Codes

M codes:
M03 P11 - main spindle rotation
M03 P12 - milling spindle rotation
M03 P13 - sub-spindle rotation
M07 - HP coolant on
M08 - coolant ON
M09 - coolant OFF
M20 - loader call
M23 - program start M-code
M24 - conveyor start
M25 - conveyor stop
M30 - Program end and rewind
M32 - guideway grease 1 min
M46 - Traction bar advance and tailstock body unclamp
M47 - Traction bar retract and tailstock body clamp
M52 - Front door open
M53 - Front door close
M55 - Repeat program after M02/03. No need to hit Cycle Start
M78 - Tailstock Quill advance
M79 - Tailstock Quill retract
M98 PXXXX - Sub-program call out
