M codes

M52 - loader door open
M53 - loader door close
M176 - clamp part
M177 - unclamp part

Tool Order

T01 - 2.5" Face Mill
T07 - Wire Brush
T03 - Rear Caliper Boss Face Mill
T11 - 4" Dia. Reg Back Brush

General Info

If the part has run and stayed untouched (not disrupted the part-present-sensor), then resetting the program and running the cycle again would only unclamp and open the loader door for the robot to swap parts.
